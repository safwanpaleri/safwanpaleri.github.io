I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Games Technology from Abertay University, Scotland, UK. I have completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering (Honors.) with minor in Machine Learning and elective in Robotic Process Automation from Lovely Professional University, Punjab, India. From a young age, I have been passionate about games, whether its completing whole mission in Grand theft auto or Watch Dogs, surving in Last of Us or Days Gone, getting lost in gripping story telling of Red dead redemption or Ghost of Tsushima, or etc. My current favorite game of all time is Sekiro: Shadow dies twice.

Professionally, I've worked as a Unity developer at App Mechanic for over two years, where I was part of development of 2D, 3D, AR, XR and VR games and application. I have worked as frontend or backend developer as per project requirement, asset creation for unity asset store. Worked with many technologies like Firebase, Unity Photon, AR Foundation, Vuforia, Mapbox, Playfab, AWS and Azure.I’ve also completed a virtual experience program with Electronic Arts, where I got hands-on with game programming in Python and C++, Unreal Engine, and cybersecurity fundamentals. Before that, I have worked as Unity Game Developer intern at Tentaxaur Studios where i got to work on gameplay and UI for several prototypes. And I have also worked as Game tester for a game.

While game development and playing games are my first and foremost passions, I’m also a big fan of movies, series, and anime. These hobbies fuel my creativity and broaden my perspective as a developer.
